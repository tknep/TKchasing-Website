About Thomas

An avid runner and adventurer, I travel throughout the United States searching for Mother Nature's most powerful storms. My goal is to document nature's beauty whether it be from the clouds or on the surface.

The world is a beautiful place, so why not share it with you. With over six years in photography and three years of storm chasing I am actively chasing my dream of being a photographer. Follow my photography passion here!
